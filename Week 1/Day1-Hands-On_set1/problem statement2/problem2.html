About Punjabi Tadka

Punjabi Tadka brings you the rich and authentic flavors of Punjab, prepared with traditional recipes and a touch of homemade love
Our dishes are made using fresh ingredients, aromatic spices, and classic Punjabi cooking techniques.

From buttery curries to sizzling tandoori delights, every meal at Punjabi Tadka is a celebration of taste, warmth, and hospitality.
We aim to give our customers a true Punjabi food experience with quality, hygiene, and great service.
